Uneo Pressure App

Uneo App is a specialty app for medical care specialists and nurses who need to monitor the muscle pressure of patients laying down all the time.

Date

14 Aug 2023 - 21 Sep 2023

Client

UCCTW

Tools

Figma, Figma Prototype, Photoshop

Prototype Link @Whatsapp @Line

Project Background

I met Carlos on online freelance work platform and it was my luck to have such an opportunity to work with them. They were trying to create a mockup UI for their body pressure monitoring app so that they could create a demo app for their customers. After a online meeting, we started with the style preferences.

Stylescape options

From the 1st meeting, I have gathered some descriptions of the desired graphic design, such as "high-tect", "professional". So I went go to create 3 stylescapes that shared those keywords while differentiate itself with some design sparks and styles.
