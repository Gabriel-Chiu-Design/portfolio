Background

Mollifix is a Taiwan local female fashion brand. They were creating a new series of underwears with artist promotion. The theme is call "Move Now", which promotes the idea of living your life with strength and impacts. It immediately reminds me of some famous sport brands like Nike, Addidas, New Balance. Therefore, we go ahead with sporty feelings.
