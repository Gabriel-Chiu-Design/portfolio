專屬的開發流程

這是我們的旅程

探索階段

We start the journey by opening a map, search for the locations with hidden treasures and stunning attractions. We seek agreements and directions at this stage

  • Clarifying the general need of the design
  • Defining the problem and possible design solutions
  • Discussing any particular preferences

概念發想與設定

We continue our adventure plan with more solid ideas, such as tone, graphic style, design concepts, functionality and design messages.

  • Clarifying the general need of the design
  • Defining the problem and possible design solutions
  • Discussing any style preferences
  • Determining the overall design style and tone
  • Creating moodboards, stylescapes and wireframes as our design blueprint

美術設計與修改

With confirmed design directions and guides, we jump into the design and adventure! A pretty and balance design draft shall be the final deliverable.

  • Adding styles and elements to make it pop
  • Applying a consistent design system over the design
  • Refining details and making adjustments

開發與製作

With the final design draft on hand, we start making it real on the internet.

  • Deciding which development tools we are going to use
  • Setting up website domain and hosting services
  • Styling the website according to our pretty design draft

測試與上架

We would go through everything again and see if there are any bugs across devices and operating system. Adjust if there are any, or time to celebrate!

  • Cross checking the website responsiveness across phone, ipad, and desktop devices
  • Confirming the content and system maintenance terms
  • Let's celebrate!
